Team Functions

The Xbox Live team functions include the following.

Function Description
XOnlineTeamCreate

Creates a team and assigns the initial team member.
XOnlineTeamCreateGetResults

Retrieves the resulting team information generated by calling XOnlineTeamCreate.
XOnlineTeamDelete

Deletes a team.
XOnlineTeamEnumerate

Retrieves team details for a list of known team XUID structures.
XOnlineTeamEnumerateByUserXUID

Enumerates a list of teams that a player belongs to.
XOnlineTeamEnumerateGetResults

Retrieves the enumeration results.
XOnlineTeamGetDetails

Retrieves information about a particular team.
XOnlineTeamMemberAnswerRecruit

Answers a recruiting request made by XOnlineTeamMemberRecruit or XOnlineTeamMemberRecruitByName.
XOnlineTeamMemberGetDetails

Retrieves information about a particular team member.
XOnlineTeamMemberRecruit

Submits a request to recruit a team member.
XOnlineTeamMemberRecruitByName

Submits a request to recruit a team member by gamertag.
XOnlineTeamMemberRemove

Removes a team member from a team.
XOnlineTeamMembersEnumerate

Enumerates a team's members.
XOnlineTeamMembersEnumerateGetResults

Retrieves information resulting from a call to XOnlineTeamMembersEnumerate.
XOnlineTeamMemberSetProperties

Sets the properties for a team member.
XOnlineTeamSetFamilyTitleID

Sets the family title ID for teams.
XOnlineTeamSetProperties

Sets the properties for a team.
