Handling Network Outages

Round progression is halted and player check-in is blocked during scheduled Xbox Live service maintenance. If the outage spans a period longer than three rounds, the tournament is automatically canceled. Otherwise, the rounds affected by the outage are rescheduled for the next available time slots. For example, if an all-day outage on Wednesday prevents the Wednesday round of a three-round Monday/Wednesday/Friday tournament from taking place, then the Wednesday round is pushed out to Friday, and the Friday round is pushed out to Monday. During the outage the player check-in returns the E_SERVER_OUTAGE error. Please direct the user to check the Xbox Live service status on www.xbox.com/live.
