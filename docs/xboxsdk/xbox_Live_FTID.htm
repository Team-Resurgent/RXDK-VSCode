Families of Titles

Xbox Live provides publishers with the ability to perform certain tasks across multiple titles. To do so, publishers group their games into one or more families of titles by assigning them the same family title ID. Titles with the same family title ID can access certain Xbox Live features across all titles in the family. For example, players can form teams on Xbox Live and play as a team in all of your family titles that support teams. This saves them from having to re-create the team for each of your titles.

The services that use family title IDs for cross-title functionality are teams, messaging and storage. For more information on using family title IDs, see the overviews for those services.
