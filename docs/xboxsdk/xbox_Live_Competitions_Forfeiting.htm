Forfeiting

A player who learns in advance that he/she must forfeit the next match should forfeit the match ahead of time. By forfeiting a match instead of simply not showing up, a player avoids the no-show penalty to his/her competitor profile. A player whose opponent forfeits the match automatically advances to the next round.

To forfeit, your title calls the XOnlineCompetitionManageEntrant funtion and passes the value XONLINE_COMP_ACTION_FORFEIT in the dwAction parameter.

Players can also withdraw from the competition entirely. To withdraw, your title calls XOnlineCompetitionManageEntrant and passes the value XONLINE_COMP_ACTION_WITHDRAW in the dwAction parameter. However, players can only withdraw before the first match of a competition begins. After that, they must forfeit.
