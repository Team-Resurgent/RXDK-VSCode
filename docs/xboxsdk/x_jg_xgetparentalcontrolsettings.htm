XGetParentalControlSetting

The XGetParentalControlSetting function returns the level of parental control set in the Dashboard.

DWORD XGetParentalControlSetting();

Parameters

None.

Return Values

The function will return one of the values below:

Value Meaning
XC_PC_ESRB_ALL All games may be played.
XC_PC_ESRB_ADULT All games with an ESRB rating of Adult, or less, may be played.
XC_PC_ESRB_MATURE All games with an ESRB rating of Mature, or less, may be played.
XC_PC_ESRB_TEEN All games with an ESRB rating of Teen, or less, may be played.
XC_PC_ESRB_EVERYONE All games with an ESRB rating of Everyone, or less, may be played.
XC_PC_ESRB_KIDS_TO_ADULTS All games with an ESRB rating of Kids to Adults, or less, may be played.
XC_PC_ESRB_EARLY_CHILDHOOD Only games with an ESRB rating of Early Childhood may be played.

Remarks

The Parental Control option on the Dashboard is available only in the North American game region. In all other regions, this function will always return the value XC_PC_ESRB_ALL.

Ratings and Title Development

If you have enabled parental control in the Xbox Dashboard on your XDK, your title may not be allowed to run. To correct this, you can disable parental control, or you can build your XBE using the /TESTRATINGS switch to specify a particular rating from the XC_PC_ESRB_xxx flags in xbox.h. For instance, XC_PC_ESRB_EARLY_CHILDHOOD corresponds to "6". So building your XBE using /TESTRATINGS:6 would cause your title to act as though it had an "Early Childhood" rating, allowing it to run under any parental control setting.

If you forget the parental control password you have assigned to your XDK, it is possible to override the password and get into the Parental Control menu by using the Xbox Controller to enter a special bypass code.

To override the parental control password:

  1. Launch the Xbox Dashboard.
  2. Select Settings, and then press the A controller button.
  3. Select Parental Control, and then press the A button.
  4. Press the X button, press the Y button, press the left trigger, and finally press the X button again.
