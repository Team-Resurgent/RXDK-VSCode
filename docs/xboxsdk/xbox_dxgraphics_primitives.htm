Direct3D Primitives for Xbox

The Xbox supports a broader range of 3D primitives than Direct3D for Win32. These are as follows.

  • Point Lists
  • Line Lists
  • Line Strips
  • Triangle Lists
  • Triangle Strips
  • Triangle Fans
  • Quadrilateral Lists
  • Quadrilateral Strips
  • Polygons
