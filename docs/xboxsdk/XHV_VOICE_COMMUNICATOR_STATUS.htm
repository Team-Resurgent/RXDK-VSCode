XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS

Indicates the status of the voice communicator.

typedef enum _XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS{
    XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS_INSERTED = 0,
    XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS_REMOVED,
} XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS, *PXHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS;

Constants

XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS_INSERTED
Indicates that the voice communicator has been inserted into the port.
XHV_VOICE_COMMUNICATOR_STATUS_REMOVED
Indicates that the voice communicator has been removed from the port.
