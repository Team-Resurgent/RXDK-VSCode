Sample Game Walk-Through


Flight Adventure Game Mission 1: Temple of Mystery

Mission Objectives

  1. Locate and fly to a remote group of islands.
  2. Take out the troop truck carrying the anti-aircraft cannon.
  3. Land on the atoll located in the lagoon and drop off the archaeologists.
  4. Protect the archaeologists from incoming enemies' aircraft.
  5. Return to base.

Mission Phases

  • Leave from your base heading west toward the small group of islands you can see on your map and radar.
  • Once you reach the east side of the islands, fly down into the southern-most canyon. There are two enemy troop transports and a fuel truck located on the road below. Maintain your altitude until the last moment, then fly low and either shoot the machine gun at the fuel truck or drop one of your two bombs onto the troop transport. Destroying both transports will stop the troops from setting up the anti-aircraft cannon, allowing you to pass through the canyon safely at the end of the mission.
  • Continue flying east until you reach the western lagoon. You will see an island and atoll in the middle of the lagoon. This is where the artifact is located. Land the plane on the small runway to drop off the archaeologists.
  • A group of three (3) enemy planes will be coming in from the north. Take off from the runway and head north to intercept them before they reach the lagoon.
  • Use one of your two missiles to take out the lead. Then lead the other two planes north and away from the island. Duck into the canyon walls located there, heading down close to the river. When you near the end of the canyon, you will see loose rocks dropping debris into the canyon. Fire a missile at the rocks and use the turbo button to fly past the falling rocks, letting them take out the two enemy aircraft behind you.
  • Return to the atoll in the western lagoon, landing on the runway to retrieve the archaeologists.
  • Return to the base the way you came; otherwise, you will encounter three other enemy aircraft at each of the northern- and southern-most canyons.

Enemies Encountered on this Level

  • 3 trucks (1 fuel and 2 troop transports)
  • 1 anti-aircraft cannon (unless both troop transports are destroyed before deployment)
  • 9 enemy planes (type A-73s)

Secrets/Hidden Items

Ancient fuel formula—drop both bombs on the ancient structure at south end of runway. This must be done in two separate bombing runs with each bomb a direct hit on the structure. The archeologist will then "discover" the formula in the ruins uncovered by the bombs.

Approximate Time to Complete Mission

15 minutes.

Awards/Rewards

After level completion, players are automatically awarded the following upgrades:

  • New higher-caliber machine gun (30mm)
  • Increased fuel tank size (+ 50%)

After level completion, the following upgrade will be awarded if the secret objective was met:

  • Fortified fuel (+ 15 power/speed)
