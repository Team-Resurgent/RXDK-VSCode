ShadowBuffer Sample

The ShadowBuffer sample illustrates how to do shadow buffering on the Xbox.

Overview

If the projected q goes negative, the shadow buffer lookup is undefined—in this sample, it shows up as black. We therefore clamp q.w to 0. You will also see black if q is projected beyond the far clip plane of the light.
