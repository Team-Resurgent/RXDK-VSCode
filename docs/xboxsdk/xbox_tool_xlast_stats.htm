Creating Stats Configurations

Stats configuration submissions enable you to upload information for the Xbox Live statistics service. The information is displayed in leaderboards that players on Xbox Live can view. This information can also be made available on the Web.

Leaderboards can have attachments, if you so desire. They are submitted as part of your stats configuration package.
