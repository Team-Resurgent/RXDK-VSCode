XACTMarkers Sample

The XACTMarkers sample demonstrates how to use XACT markers and notifications. This samples used markers placed in content, by the content creator, in the wave file, on each word. The markers are then detected by the sample through the use of notifications, and the word corresponding to the spoken word from the wave file is then shown on the screen.
