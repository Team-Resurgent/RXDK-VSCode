PlayField Sample

The PlayField sample draws a grassy sports field.

Overview

The grass is drawn back-to-front using a series of "fins". The grass strip texture is sampled from geometry as a pre-process step. The grass side-view texture is modulated by the underlying field texture to get stripes and rough spots. When used in a sports game, the field texture can be updated dynamically to get shadows of the ball and players.
