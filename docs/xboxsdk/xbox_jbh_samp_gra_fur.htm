Fur Sample

The Fur sample demonstrates a technique for rendering hair or fur on an object. The fur is rendered using concentric shells of a volume texture. As the objects get further away, shells are blended together and combined to improve rendering speed. This sample also demonstrates how the fur can be deformed; for instance, by a blow dryer.

Controls

For a complete list of controller mappings, press the Back button on the Xbox controller while this sample is running.
